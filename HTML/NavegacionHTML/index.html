Bienvenido a Hole

Hole es una localizacion muy industrializada, con muchas cosas por realizar en ella. This is why it has been categorized as a megacity, which is a very large city with a population of thousands of people. This, of course, gives The Hole a lot going for it which means that there is a lot for this very large population to do.

Since the beginning of mankind, the Magic Users came to the Hole in order to test their magic on humans, killing or disfiguring them. Thanks to their constant attacks, Hole became a hideous and dangerous place for everyone. The overall population of the city lives in poor conditions, most of them struggling to live with the burdens imposed by the sorcerers' magic on their bodies and the constant fear of being used as guinea pigs.

The buildings also show signs of extensive deterioration, with several areas of the city consisting of disheveled buildings, as if the entire city is one massive slum. The overall look of the city is a field of buildings and dark corridors. The city was created around the Lake of Refuse.

The rain which falls on the Hole is caused by the residues of magic smoke that are in the sky, causing a thin layer of black dust to accumulate with the falling rain. The rain is toxic to magic users, as the residue contained within the rain causes harm to their bodies. For example, the rain causes Nikaido to become so sick that she was unable to leave her house.

Hungry Bug

Hungry Bug is a restaurant owned by Nikaido located in a outlying district of Hole, close to the infamous passage where Kaiman was found. The area is well known to be frequented by the Magic Users, and due to this, customers tend to be rare.

Hungry Bug is a dirty, small building that's partly infested with cockroaches. The interior of the kitchen has big pipes running near the ceiling with multiple pork bodies and other various cuts of meat hooked onto the pipes.

The Hospital

Created after a young Shin annihilated Hole's militia and the humans gave up trying to defend themselves against Magic Users, because of that, the Magic Users trips to Hole increased so much that the city has to make a special sector for people injured or morphed by magic, mostly just prolonging their inevitable death or poorly improve their lives. Most of the patients come in critical condition and because there is no cure to magic (except by killing the sorcerer who curse them), victims live the rest of their lives in that condition.

Prof. Kasukabe usually goes to the Hospital with Jonson to keep Vaux company (even though Jonson could not be able to stay there due the fact he is a mutated giant cockroach). The Hospital was took over by the remains of the En Family when they scaped from the Magic User realm due sudden the apocalyptic rain, and helping Vaux with the daily chores like cleaning, making coffee and bringing the newspaper to him (he was terrorized by their presence but got used to it). Vaux tried to get rid the Family Goons with the notice that the top members of the organization died in the sinking of the Central Department Store, the notice was actually effective making the goons storm out the hospital.

Central Department Store

The Central Department Store is a commercial building in Hole erected over the reclaimed land of "The Lake of Refuse" and centered primarily in the latest arc of the manga, playing an important role in the climax of the story.
